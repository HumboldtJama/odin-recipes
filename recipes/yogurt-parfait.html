A yogurt parfait aimed to please

Looking for an easy macro meal? This is great for breakfast or a quick meal before you head out the door

Get your ingredients ready:

  • low fat greek yogurt
  • blueberries
  • strawberries
  • granola
  • chia seeds
  • flax seed
  • organic maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Grab your bowl and 1/3c low fat greek yogurt
  2. Add 1 tsp chia seeds
  3. Add 1 tsp flax seed
  4. Add 1 tbsp organic maple syrup
  5. Now mix those ingredients real good until even distrubited
  6. Add 1/4c washed blueberries
  7. Add 1/4c quartered and washed strawberries
  8. Now add 1/4c of your favorite granola
  9. Enjoy the layered aesthetic, then mix and enjoy!
