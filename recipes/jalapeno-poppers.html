Best homemade jalapeno poppers!

This spicy comfort will entertain all those involved! Want some spice and some gooey cheese? Try these poppers out, they wont disappoint!

Lets find those ingredients:

  • jalapenos
  • cream cheese
  • shredded sharp cheddar
  • chives

Now put it together:

  1. Grab a baking sheet and line it with foil
  2. Wash and halve jalapenos
  3. Deseed jalapenos and place on baking sheet
  4. In a bowl mix 8oz cream cheese, 1/4 shreeded sharp cheddar cheese, and 3 diced chive(only the green parts)
  5. Once mixed scoop and fill jalapenos
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 16 minutes
  7. Once jalapenos are tender pull them out
  8. Serve warm and enjoy!
